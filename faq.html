Frequently asked questions

General

Where can I park my car?

Stratford International car park is a 1-minute walk away.

Do you have gym facilities?

Yes, we have a fully-equipped in-house gym available.

Do you have Wi-Fi?

Yes, we have fast, free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.

Do you have wheelchair access?

Yes, wheelchair users can access our accommodation, bars, restaurants and market. Our King Accessible rooms are designed specifically for people with limited mobility. You can find details of the room here.

Find us

We’re next to Stratford International station, with regular fast connections around the city. The Eurostar at King’s Cross St. Pancras is a 7-minute train. And if you’re flying, London City Airport is just 20 minutes away.
