More than a place to rest your head

While each of our 291 rooms has been elegantly designed to cater for all your creature comforts, The Gantry is so much more than just a place to rest and recuperate. What makes it an extraordinary stay is that you can also immerse yourself in a wide assortment of outstanding food, drink and entertainment as you soak in the vibrancy of this very special neighbourhood.

Discover Flavour

No matter where you order meals and snacks in The Gantry, your tastebuds will be in for a real treat. This is a result of our expert team of grocers and chefs working closely together using freshly and responsibly sourced ingredients to prepare their culinary creations. It’s a philosophy that results in our guests and visitors enjoying excellent products throughout The Gantry, from the street-level cafés and bars, to room service, to the rooftop restaurant and terrace on the 18th floor.

Learn More

A community alive

Where kerb-side creativity collides with world-class cultural institutions. Explore a food and drink scene that’s forever evolving – from fine dining to imaginative street food. Browse your way through one-off boutiques and top international brands. Or let yourself be inspired by the street art, galleries, film and music.

Learn More

A collaboration of the like-minded

Our unique home in Stratford is where creative and talented minds come together to collaborate, explore and discover. This culture of collaboration doesn’t just run in our veins, but also in everything we do from food to cocktails to art shows to live entertainment. Whether you’re staying as a guest in one of our 291 stylish rooms, or if you’ve just joined us for a sundowner on the rooftop terrace, you’ll discover something to inspire you on each of The Gantry’s 18 floors.

Learn More

Close to it all

With Stratford International station on our doorstep, it’s easy to get in, out or around. Fast tube and train connections put the best of the city within touching distance, while nearby London City Airport offers international flights.
